General Medicine

Kayachikitsa referred to as internal medicine forms the foundation of this ancient holistic healing system. It is a branch of Ayurveda that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases affecting the internal organs and systems of the body. Rooted in Ayurveda principles, Kayachikitsa focuses on restoring balance and vitality to the body through personalized treatments tailored to your unique needs. It encompasses a wide range of therapeutic approaches, including Herbal medicines and formulations, dietary and lifestyle recommendations, Panchakarma (detoxification therapies), and Rasayana (Rejuvenation therapies).

Kaumarabhritya

Ayurveda Pediatrics or Kaumarbhritya is a branch of Ayurveda dedicated to Child healthcare through all the formative stages of childhood.

Healthy growth and development through infancy till 16 years of age, ensuring healthy breastfeeding, nutrition centric diet guidance during every stage of childhood as well as disease specific prescription diets ensures an all-round comprehensive care for children of all ages.

Ayurveda pediatrics blends ancient wisdom with modern Pediatric care to offer holistic and natural treatments for health and wellbeing of children. It emphasizes a comprehensive approach to pediatric care, focusing on the physical, mental, and emotional growth of children

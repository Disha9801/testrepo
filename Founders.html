THE MAN WHO STARTED IT ALL

DR. MS RAMAIAH

FOUNDER

(1922 - 1997)

Well-aware of the instrumental role formal education played in the progress of society.

He wished for others what he wished for himself. Thus, empathy

became the foundation of the organization desire to epitomise “Inclusive Excellence”

Under the aegis of Gokula Education Foundation (GEF) Trust he established Ramaiah Institute of Technology in 1962, The Ramaiah Medical College in 1979 and the Medical College Hospital in 1984

over the next decade several other institutions were setup strengthening the Educational Focus of the Group.
